Features

Project Management

Create and manage projects with ease. With our project management card, you can create new projects, view existing projects, and organize them based on your needs. Each project card includes a deadline to help you stay on track and ensure that you complete the project on time.

Task Management

Create and manage tasks within your projects. Our task management card allows you to break down your projects into smaller, manageable tasks. You can assign deadlines to each task, add descriptions, and even create subtasks to help you stay organized.

Collaboration

Invite team members to collaborate on projects and tasks. Our collaboration card makes it easy to work with your team members and ensure that everyone is on the same page. You can invite team members to specific projects, assign tasks to them, and even communicate with them directly from the card.
